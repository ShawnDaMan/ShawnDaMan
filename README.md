## Usage

1. **Add a Vehicle**: Fill out the form on the left with vehicle details and click "Add Vehicle"
2. **View Fleet**: See all your vehicles in the fleet inventory table
3. **Change Status**: Click the "Status" button to cycle through available, in-use, and maintenance states
4. **Delete Vehicle**: Click "Delete" to remove a vehicle from the fleet
5. **Filter**: Use the status filter dropdown to show only specific vehicle statuses
